## Premier League App

This project implements a simulation. In this simulation, there is a group of
football teams and the simulation show match results and the league table. Estimated
the final league table. In addition, after the 4th week, estimation is also represented on screen.

## League Rules:

 - There is four teams in the league.
 - Other rules in the league (scoring, points, goal difference, etc.) is the same as the rules of the
Premier League.

http://www.premierleague.com/en-gb/matchday/league-table.html

## Extras

 - After you click on the all-League Play button, the matches will play automatically until the end of the
League and list the results of the matches by weeks.
 - You can edit the results of the matches and calculate the edited results of the matches based on the
   content of the modified standings.
